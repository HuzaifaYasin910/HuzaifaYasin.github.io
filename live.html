VISIT NOVELS & NOTIONS
"Novels & Notions" is one of my live archived projects, and its core concept revolves around the world of literature and reading. It serves as both an online book publishing platform and a digital bookstore. This project is designed to cater to the needs of avid readers, authors, and book enthusiasts alike, providing a unique space where literature and technology converge.

Although it was a side project .this project was a delightful journey, a testament to the idea that personal passion projects can be just as rewarding and fulfilling as professional endeavors. It provided a space where I could blend my creative and technical talents, resulting in a platform that celebrates the joy of reading and writing, all while reminding me of the fun that can be found in the world of coding and digital creation.
